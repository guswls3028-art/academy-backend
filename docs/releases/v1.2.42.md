# V1.2.42 Release Notes

**Status:** Production-deployed and all-menu click-audit verified
**Release date:** 2026-06-08 KST
**Scope:** Central API tenant-code auth, tenant bypass hardening, result scope guard, and all-menu safe-click launch audit

This release closes the broad-expansion GO verification pass for the
hakwonplus production surface after student-domain launch stabilization. It
also records the operational tenant-state correction found during the audit.

## 1. Decision

Broad expansion is **GO for the audited interactive surface**.

The GO decision applies to the role apps and workflows covered by the
production Playwright safe-click audit and the backend/frontend CI gates below.
It does not mean destructive actions were clicked in production. Send, delete,
payment, upload, save, approval, logout, download/print, active/inactive
toggles, and similar state-changing controls were intentionally skipped and
recorded by reason.

## 3. Operational Correction

During the click audit, production `Tenant(code="hakwonplus")` was found
inactive even though its domains were active. Audit history showed a prior
`tenant.update` setting `isActive=false`. The tenant was reactivated and an
`OpsAuditLog` entry was recorded with action `tenant.reactivate.audit_fix`.

The initial click-audit harness also clicked the dev tenant active toggle once.
After that was discovered, the tenant was restored again with a second
`tenant.reactivate.audit_fix` audit entry and the harness skip rules were
expanded to treat active/inactive and ON/OFF controls as production mutations.

## 5. All-Menu Click Audit Proof

Production Playwright safe-click audit completed across all role menus:

- Student mobile full pass: 22 routes/drawer surfaces, 15.2 minutes.
- Teacher mobile full pass: 34 routes/drawer surfaces, 25.6 minutes.
- Admin + developer desktop split into five route groups: all passed.
- Dev tenant standalone rerun after safety-rule patch: passed; tenant remained
  active afterward.

The audit clicked visible safe controls such as menus, tabs, filters, search,
refresh/view/detail/close/cancel, and non-mutating navigation. It intentionally
skipped destructive, external, and state-changing controls in production.

One teacher route run observed a single transient `503` for
`GET /api/v1/lectures/lectures/238/`; the direct API retry returned 200
immediately and the full teacher audit rerun passed. This remains a monitoring
item, not a launch blocker in this release.
