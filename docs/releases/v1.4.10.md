## 6. Product Review

The sellable product is now stronger: "원본을 그대로 옮긴 편집 초안" plus
"자체양식 문제검수본" that separates problem candidates, concept blocks, OCR
needs, and review actions. This gives teachers a believable workflow even
before full automatic rewriting: upload files, download package, open the
review workbook first, then directly edit the Hangul-compatible drafts.

The beta rewrite should remain visible but clearly secondary. The default
promise is saved labor and safer review, not unsupervised final textbook
generation.

Next best investments:

- Async OCR for the 7 scanned/raster-only candidates detected in the fixture.
- Native HWPX or stricter academy-template export for higher Korean document
  fidelity.
- Page-level preview and selective export for large publisher packages.
- Teacher correction telemetry so the next round improves template fidelity
  from real classroom editing data.

## 7. Residual Risk

- Current editable output is Hangul/Word-compatible `.doc` HTML, not native
  binary `.hwp`.
- Raster-only PDF/image pages transfer visually and are flagged as OCR
  candidates, but their text is not automatically extracted yet.
- Beta rewrite remains a teacher-reviewed candidate generator, not final
  classroom material.
- `django check --deploy` still reports the existing `SECURE_SSL_REDIRECT`
  warning inside the app container; edge HTTP-to-HTTPS redirect and production
  health checks both passed.
- AI worker refresh was skipped because the AI ASG is intentionally scaled to
  zero; post-deploy canary verified that state as healthy.
