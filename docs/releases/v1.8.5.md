# V1.8.5 Release Notes

**Status:** Production-deployed / GO

**Release date:** 2026-07-19 KST

**Scope:** Verified school/mock score submissions, cumulative score operations
console, student filtering, and production UX closure

## 1. Decision

GO for production use.

Academy tests, school semester exams, nationwide mock exams, and KICE mock
assessments can now be managed as separate score sources. Students submit one
subject and its original report per record; staff approval is required before
the score enters statistics. Backend and frontend deployments succeeded, the
post-deploy canary passed with `PASS=30 WARN=0 FAIL=0`, and independent
deployment verification returned `PASS / GO`.

## 2. Product Contract

- The student app accepts school exams by academic year, semester, and first or
  second written-exam round; nationwide mocks accept a month; KICE mocks are
  restricted to the official June and September periods.
- A submission contains one subject. This keeps score scales, evidence, review,
  and correction replacement unambiguous. Students repeat the same short flow
  for additional subjects on a multi-subject report.
- School records support 5-grade and 9-grade systems, achievement A-E, subject
  average, standard deviation, and cohort size. A grade cannot be stored without
  an explicit grade system.
- Uploaded image/PDF evidence is tenant- and student-bound, signature checked,
  protected from delete/overwrite while linked, and remains pending until a
  staff member verifies or rejects it.
- Only verified records contribute to analytics. A verified correction replaces
  the previous verified record for the same student/exam/subject key.

## 3. Operations Console and UX

- The results tab separates overall coverage, academy tests, school records, and
  mocks instead of averaging incompatible sources into one business metric.
- Period, direct student, name/school/lecture search, lecture, grade, subject,
  score band, trend, and sort controls can be combined. Deferred search keeps a
  large student roster responsive.
- Each student row shows source coverage; the detail panel shows subject-specific
  cumulative line charts and exact period-matched history. Pending student
  reports remain in a dedicated original-view/verify/reject queue.
- The student submission flow was reorganized into exam type, score fields, and
  original upload steps, with recent submission states and retryable loading
  errors. Desktop and mobile layouts were visually reviewed.

## 4. Data and Security

- Migration `results.0013_studentreportedscore` adds the tenant-scoped submitted
  score and evidence-review record.
- Student identity, tenant, evidence scope, submitting user, and reviewer role
  are validated at the write boundary. Cross-tenant review returns not found.
- Student-first row locking serializes competing corrections without introducing
  an inconsistent lock order.
- No production score row or E2E residue was created during release validation.
  Infrastructure topology, messaging policy, and worker protocols did not
  change.
