# V1.2.14 Release Notes

**Status:** production-deployed
**Release date:** 2026-06-01 KST
**Scope:** tchul OMR late-result recovery — hydrate AI answers after manual student matching and regrade affected submissions

This hotfix closes the June 1 tchul OMR recurrence where the teacher
manually matched students while the AI worker was still processing. The
old idempotency guard treated those submissions as already done, so the
late AI answers were ignored and scores stayed at zero.

## 1. OMR Late AI Answer Hydration

- `apply_omr_ai_result` now has a narrow recovery path for OMR submissions
  that are already `answers_ready` or `done`, have a manually selected
  `enrollment_id`, have no `SubmissionAnswer` rows yet, and receive a
  worker result with answers.
- In that case, the callback persists AI answers, preserves the manual
  student match, moves `done -> answers_ready` with an explicit override
  actor, and lets the normal grading callback finish the score update.
- Existing idempotency remains unchanged for submissions that already have
  answers, lack a manual enrollment, are `grading`, or are superseded.

## 2. Tests

- Added a regression test for the exact incident shape: a manually matched
  `done` OMR with zero answers receives a late AI callback, hydrates answers,
  and grades against `Exam.max_score` fallback when question scores are zero.
- Updated the standalone OMR pipeline script check to look at the current
  `answer_persister.py` SSOT after the Phase C refactor.
